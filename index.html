March 9th, 2018

Hosted by the Scholars' Lab at the UVA Library

March 9th, 2018 the Scholars' Lab at the University of Virginia Library will host students, faculty, and staff from the University of Maryland, the University of Richmond, and Washington and Lee University in March 2018 to discuss overlaps in their digital humanities programs and potential ways to collaborate.
